Frequently Asked Questions

Navigating Your Therapy Journey
How does the therapy process work?

The therapy process begins with an initial session to get to know you, assess your needs, and set therapy goals. Following this, regular sessions start based on the established goals.

How long are sessions and how often are they held?

A typical therapy session lasts 50 minutes. The frequency may vary, but most clients prefer weekly sessions. As you progress, the intervals may be reduced.

How long should I continue therapy?

The duration depends on your personal needs, goals, and progress. Some people attend for a few months, while others continue longer. We regularly evaluate to find the best path for you.

What platforms are used for online therapy sessions?

We conduct therapy sessions using various platforms such as WhatsApp, Zoom, and Google Meet. This flexibility allows you to choose the most convenient option for you.

Do you offer free trial sessions?

Yes, we offer a free trial session for new clients. Please contact us to schedule your session.

What are the fees and payment options?

For fees and payment options, please refer to the Plans & Pricing section or contact us. We accept all major credit and debit cards.

How is confidentiality maintained in therapy?

All therapy processes are confidential. Information shared in sessions is only disclosed with your consent and is protected under professional ethical guidelines.

How should I prepare for the first session?

No specific preparation is needed. Simply try to feel comfortable and think about topics you wish to discuss. We’re here to get to know you and start the process.

Do I need a mental health diagnosis to participate in therapy?

No diagnosis is required to begin therapy. Whether you need emotional support or simply want to better understand yourself, we are here to help you.

What topics do you cover in therapy?

We address a variety of issues, such as relationship problems, childhood trauma, addictions, grief, depression, anxiety, stress management, anger control, and career issues.

What if I struggle to express myself during a session?

This is very common, and we are here to help. Expressing oneself can be challenging, but we’ll work together with patience and understanding to help you progress in this area.

Billing and Payment Queries
What are the fees for therapy sessions?

The fees for therapy sessions vary based on the type and duration of the session. Please refer to the Plans & Pricing section for detailed information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and online payment platforms. Please check with us for specific options.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans to make therapy accessible. Please contact us for more details on setting up a payment plan that works for you.

Is insurance accepted?

We accept various insurance plans. Please contact your insurance provider or us directly to check coverage details for therapy services.

Can I get a refund if I cancel my session?

Refund policies may vary. Typically, cancellations made with adequate notice may be eligible for a refund or rescheduling. Please review our cancellation policy for specifics.

Are there any additional fees I should be aware of?

Additional fees may apply for extended sessions or specific services. Please check our Plans & Pricing section for a complete breakdown.

How do I set up payment for my sessions?

Setting up payment is simple. You can provide your payment information during your first session or contact us to set it up in advance.

Will I receive a receipt for my payment?

Yes, you will receive a receipt for your payment after each session, which can be used for insurance claims or personal records.

Can I change my payment method later?

Yes, you can change your payment method by contacting us. Just let us know your preferred method, and we will update it for future sessions.

How will I be notified about payment due dates?

You will receive notifications about payment due dates via email or text reminders, ensuring you are always informed.

Can I pay for multiple sessions in advance?

Yes, you can pay for multiple sessions in advance. Please contact us to arrange this option and discuss any available discounts.

If you have any further questions or need assistance on any other topic, please feel free to reach out to me. I'm here to help!
